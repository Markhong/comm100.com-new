Product Update Webinar —
May 2018: Chatbot 2.0, Co-Browsing, and Shopify Integration

What’s Included in the Webinar

Join us in a live webinar to learn about our platform product updates launching May 12th:


Chatbot 2.0 — deliver efficient service with a self-learning AI powered bot that books meetings, answers questions, or helps customers make payments.

Co-Browsing — instantly view and Interact with a visitor’s web browser in a single click, making complex issue resolution easy and reducing the friction in your interactions.

Shopify Integration — convert more browsers to buyers with a seamless and personalized shopping experience.
